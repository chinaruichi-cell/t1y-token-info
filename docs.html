Project Summary

T1Y is a BNB Smart Chain protocol focused on on-chain liquidity, LP-based hashrate incentives, referral rewards, node rewards, and transparent contract-executed rules around the T1Y/WBNB pool.

The project uses verified smart contracts on BSC. Users interact with the contract and on-chain liquidity directly through their own wallets. The protocol does not guarantee price performance, profit, or principal protection.

Whitepaper Summary

  • Liquidity basis: T1Y operates around the T1Y/WBNB liquidity pool on BNB Smart Chain.
  • Participation: Eligible users can participate through contract-defined BNB deposit and LP-related flows.
  • Rewards: Reward accounting is executed by the verified contracts according to static hashrate, dynamic hashrate, referral, and node-related rules.
  • Burn mechanism: Contract-defined burn behavior is used as part of the protocol's on-chain token-economy rules.
  • Transparency: Key contracts are verified on BscScan, and owner permissions have been renounced where owner-controlled administration existed.

A fuller public whitepaper page is available at T1Y Whitepaper. It explains the contract set, participation path, reward categories, burn behavior, ownership status, and risk boundaries in one place for explorers, wallets, and users.
